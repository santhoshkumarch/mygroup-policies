Child Safety Standards Policy

Last Updated: December 2024

Our Commitment

My Group App is committed to protecting children and maintaining a safe environment for all users. We have zero tolerance for child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE) content on our platform.

Prohibited Content

The following content is strictly prohibited and will result in immediate account termination and reporting to authorities:

  • Any imagery depicting child sexual abuse or exploitation
  • Content that sexualizes minors in any way
  • Attempts to groom, solicit, or exploit minors
  • Sharing or requesting CSAE material
  • Any content that endangers children

Reporting

If you encounter any content that violates this policy, please report it immediately:

  • Use the in-app report feature on any content or profile
  • Contact us directly at: mygroupapp.web@gmail.com

Our Response

When we receive a report or detect violating content, we will:

  • Immediately remove the content
  • Permanently ban the offending account
  • Report to the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC)
  • Cooperate fully with law enforcement investigations
  • Preserve evidence as required by law

Prevention Measures

We employ the following measures to prevent CSAE on our platform:

  • Content moderation and review systems
  • User reporting mechanisms
  • Age verification requirements
  • Regular policy reviews and updates

Contact

For questions about this policy, contact us at: mygroupapp.web@gmail.com
